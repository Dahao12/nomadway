Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect your information

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide: name, email, phone, professional and immigration situation information.

We automatically collect site access information: IP address, browser, visit duration, pages accessed.

2. Use of Information

We use your information to: provide consulting and mentorship; answer your questions; improve our services; send relevant communications.

We do not share your information with third parties except when necessary to provide requested services (ex: connection with lawyers or accountants).

3. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration or destruction.

However, no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience, remember preferences and understand usage metrics.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect site functionality.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your information; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; withdraw consent for data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact: contact@nomadway.com.br

6. Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send marketing emails about our services. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking 'Unsubscribe' in the email.

We do not share your information for marketing purposes with third parties.

7. Retention Policy

We retain your information while necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

After relationship ends, we keep data only for necessary period (usually 3 years).

8. Applicable Law

This privacy policy is governed by Spanish laws (GDPR) and Brazilian laws (LGPD).

Any dispute will be resolved in competent courts.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights: contact@nomadway.com.br
