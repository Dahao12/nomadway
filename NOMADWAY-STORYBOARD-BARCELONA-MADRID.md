## Video Specifications
- **Total Duration:** 14 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio:**
  - YouTube/Website: 16:9 (1920x1080 or 3840x2160 for 4K)
  - Instagram Reels/TikTok: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Resolution:** 1080p (HD) or 4K if platform supports
- **Frame Rate:** 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps ( smoother)
- **Codec:** H.264 (MP4) for web compatibility
- **Bitrate:** 8-12 Mbps (1080p) or 20-40 Mbps (4K)

## Color Grading
- **Overall Palette:** Warm Mediterranean tones
- **Contrast:** Medium-high, cinematic
- **Saturation:** Slightly elevated (vibrant travel aesthetic)
- **Temperature:** Warm (golden hour feel throughout)

## Sound Design
- **Music:**
  - Barcelona: Coastal, upbeat Spanish guitar
  - Transition: Brief whoosh or train AVE whistle
  - Madrid: Classical strings + regal guitar
  - End: Memorable sting resolving to brand soundmark

- **Ambient Layers:** City sounds, nature (seagulls, bells), people
- **Voiceover (optional):** BBC-style travel narration (Spanish-accented English or native Spanish)
- **Mix Ratio:** Music 60%, Ambient 30%, Voiceover 10% (if used)
